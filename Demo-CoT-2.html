Scenario:
A municipal civil project is behind schedule. The Project Manager has identified two options for the next phase (tunneling):

  • Option X: Uses a new, unproven safety technology that saves 6 weeks but has a 1% chance of temporary toxic gas release (low public risk).
  • Option Y: Uses standard, slower technology (takes 6 weeks longer) but is 100% safe for the surrounding community.

The contract includes a $500,000 liquidated damages penalty if the deadline is missed.
