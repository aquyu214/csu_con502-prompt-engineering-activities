Activity – AI for CM Research Prompt Competition

Time: 15–20 minutes

Research Scenario

Imagine a first-year Construction Management (CM) graduate student who wants to use AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, etc.) to support their research. They know AI can help, but they are not sure how, where, or when to use it responsibly.

Your goal is to design a prompt that will make an AI model generate a structured, practical guide on how CM graduate students can use AI throughout the research process.

Your Task

Use any prompt engineering techniques you'd like to create a highly effective prompt, then use it to generate a research-support guide for:

  • Research planning and refining research questions
  • Literature search strategies and summarizing articles
  • Brainstorming survey/interview questions
  • Developing coding schemes and qualitative analysis
  • Understanding statistical concepts (not calculation)
  • Drafting and revising academic writing
  • Ethical and responsible use of AI in research
  • Construction-focused examples (safety, scheduling, BIM, productivity)

The final output should be a graduate-level guide: clear, structured, realistic,
