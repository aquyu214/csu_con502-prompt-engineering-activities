Activity – Visual Scenario Generation Challenge

Scenario (Real CDC FACE Case)

This activity uses a real construction accident case from the CDC FACE Program. Please review the case here:
https://stacks.cdc.gov/view/cdc/164823

Scenario summary:

An ironworker was installing rebar in a five-foot trench next to an existing cinder-block retaining wall. The trench had been excavated below the base of the retaining wall, and there was no shoring, bracing, or structural support for the adjacent wall. The retaining wall collapsed suddenly into the trench, fatally striking the worker.

Your Task

Use any prompt engineering techniques you'd like to generate an image that realistically represents this accident scenario.

You may use:

  • Zero-shot prompting
  • One-shot & few-shot prompting
  • Role prompting
  • Contextual prompting
  • Chain-of-Thought prompting
  • Automatic Prompt Engineering (APE)

Your goal is to produce the most accurate, detailed, and visually effective image.

Your workflow may include:

  • a single well-crafted prompt,
  • a sequence of prompts that build on each other,
  • follow-up prompts that refine or expand earlier outputs,
  • revisions, rewrites, critiques, or improvements,
  • different prompts focusing on different aspects of the image (scene, lighting, context, etc.).

In other words, the final image does not need to come from one single prompt. Use whatever iterative process helps you produce the best result.

Instructions

  • Read the CDC case summary.
  • Try different prompt-engineering strategies.
  • Use APE (optional) to refine and improve your prompts.
  • Generate several images and choose your best one.
  • Submit one final image for voting.

Note:
This activity involves a real fatal incident. Please engage with professionalism and respect. The purpose is to improve safety communication and hazard awareness.
